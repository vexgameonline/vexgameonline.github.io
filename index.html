Exploring the Vex Games Series: From Vex 3 to Vex 7

Vex games series offers a thrilling journey through a world of stick platformers, including titles like Vex 3, Vex 4, Vex 5, Vex 6, and Vex 7. Each installment builds upon the success of its predecessors, delivering challenging gameplay and exciting adventures.

Vex 3 kicked off the series, captivating players with its engaging levels and innovative mechanics. It set the stage for what would become a beloved franchise, paving the way for future installments.

Vex 4 continued the saga with its release in October 2018. Featuring nine acts filled with obstacles to overcome, it challenged players to test their reflexes and skills. With its cool physics and challenging levels, Vex 4 became a favorite among gaming enthusiasts.

Vex 5 introduced new features and levels, keeping players hooked with its fresh content. As the series evolved, so did the challenges, offering both newcomers and seasoned players a chance to experience the excitement of the Vex universe.

Following the success of Vex 5, Vex 6 emerged, pushing the boundaries of the series even further. With its innovative gameplay and captivating levels, it continued to captivate audiences worldwide.

Most recently, Vex 7 entered the scene, bringing with it a new set of challenges and adventures. As the series continues to grow, so does the excitement surrounding each new release, cementing its place in the world of stick platformers.
