Game Controls

In Boxrob, players can easily control their character using basic keyboard commands:

  • Move Left/Right: Press 'A' to move left and 'D' to move right.
  • Jump: Use the 'W' key to make your character jump over obstacles.
  • Pick Up Boxes: Simply tap or left-click your mouse button to collect boxes scattered throughout the levels.

Objective

The primary objective of Boxrob is to maneuver your character through each level, dodging obstacles and collecting as many boxes as possible. With each box collected, your score increases, so aim to gather them swiftly while avoiding hazards that may impede your progress.

As you embark on your Boxrob adventure, you'll encounter an array of challenges, from precarious platforms to moving obstacles.
