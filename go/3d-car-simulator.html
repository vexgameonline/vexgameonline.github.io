Game Guide

Here's a guide to the controls in 3D Car Simulator:

  • WASD or arrow keys to drive
  • Space bar to use handbrake
  • C to change camera
  • Shift to change car
  • R to reset car
  • E to turn on/off police car's lights

Mastering these controls will allow you to navigate the open world with ease, whether you're cruising through the city, drifting around corners, or engaging in high-speed pursuits. So buckle up, rev your engines, and get ready to experience the ultimate driving adventure in 3D Car Simulator!
