Embark on an exciting adventure as man's best friend in Dog Simulator 3D, a captivating browser-based game that allows you to experience the world from a canine perspective. With its immersive 3D graphics and realistic gameplay, Dog Simulator 3D offers an entertaining and interactive experience directly in your web browser.

Controls

Take control of your furry protagonist with these intuitive controls:

  • Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to navigate through various environments, exploring the world around you and interacting with objects.
  • Press Shift to break into a sprint, allowing you to cover more ground and chase after your targets with agility.
  • Tap the Space bar to leap into the air, overcoming obstacles and reaching higher vantage points to explore.
  • Engage in playful antics or defend yourself against threats by clicking the left mouse button to unleash a powerful attack.
