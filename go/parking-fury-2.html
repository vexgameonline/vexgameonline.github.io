Instructions

In Parking Fury 2, players must navigate through various obstacles and challenges to reach the designated parking space. Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to drive your car, following the arrows to guide you to the yellow parking spot. Exercise caution to avoid collisions with walls or other vehicles along the way.
